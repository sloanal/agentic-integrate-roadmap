The takeaway

By the end: an Integrate you run by asking.

One agent that summarizes the noise, understands every risk, and answers from live data with sources — then changes and builds whole schedules on command, every edit reviewed before it saves. Reachable from the app or any AI client, always on the existing permissions. The plumbing is paid for; from here, new capability is assembly, not invention.
